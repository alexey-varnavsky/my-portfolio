About Me

Let me tell you a few things...

INFO

Over the past year, I have studied frontend courses at www.udemi.com. It really fascinated me and became my new hobby. Here I want to tell you a little about what technology I learned during those courses..

HTML5

I learned about meta tags, semantic tags, how to structure a page, block and inline-level elements, divs and spans, forms and input, lists and tables, links images and attributes and even more...

CSS3

I studied about CSS selectors, box model, positioning, flexbox, targeted selectors, nth-child pseudo-selectors, before and after pseudo-selectors, CSS animation, grid, media queries and responsive design.

JavaScript

It blows my mind - almost everything in JavaScript is an object. I also learned about DOM manipulation, MVC, modules, babel, ES6, API calls, asynchronous javascript: promises async-await and AJAX, node.js, npm and yarn, classes and methods.
